About US


BMB Pumps is a leading electric water pumps manufacturer, established by the Late Haji Muhammad Anwar in 1988 in Gujranwala district of Pakistan. The current management team comprises of:
Haji Muhammad Shahid Anwar (Chief Executive and Head Research and Development)
Mr. Zubair Shahid (Technical lead and Head Marketing dept.)
Mr. Umair Shahid (Manager Production and Operation)
Mr. Uzair Shahid (Manager Finance)
We specialize in the manufacturing of electric water pumps and electric motors with a diverse range of specifications, to address needs and demands in the industrial, commercial, agricultural and residential domains. Our manufacturing site is very ideally situated in the heartland of Pakistan, in the popular industrial district of Gujranwala. We proudly possess, the capacity and training to cater to the needs of our clients all over Pakistan, and deliver quality products on demand.
BMB Pumps has more than 31 years of experience in the Pakistani Pumping Industry, and has also established an equally long commitment with its clients. Our products are best recognized as reliable, technically advanced, innovative, modernistic, highly power efficient and incredibly easy to install. Our customers enjoy the fruits of our extensive and in-depth technical expertise that distinguish us from our counterparts in the industry. When you choose BMB, you ensure environmental friendly and optimal management of your most precious resource, water.
Our equipment is widely used, some applications being those in Water Supply Systems, Cooling Systems, Water Circulation Systems, Fire-hazard solutions such as sprinklers, showering and pressurized water supply for high-rise buildings and fire fighting systems, Water Transportation, Farmland Irrigation and Drainage, and other related applications in the Agricultural and Industrial spheres.
Our team comprises of some of the best professional and technical resources in Pakistan. Technical proficiency, unparalleled efficiency, polished quality products, a secure and reliable quality control, strong and highly satisfactory after-sales services help us meet all of our clients' requests. Our actions and strategies revolve around people who have associated with us, and they remain our main focus.
We strive to deliver the best products and services to our clients. BMB Pumps has become a widely used and trusted brand all over Pakistan, and we plan to extend our reach soon. We warmly welcome all sorts of inquiries regarding our products, services and business partnerships.
